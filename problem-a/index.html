Olivia Carolyn Pope

Olivia Pope is the main character in the ABC show Scandal. She resides in Washington D.C. and is considered to be D.C.'s number one fixer and political campaign manager. Her job is to make her client's problems go away no matter the cost. In the show, she works for a variety of people going through scandals such as senators, businesspeople, and the President of the United States. Additionally, Pope gets into countless scandals herself.

3 reasons why I admire this character:

  • She is a quick problem-solver and she always wants to win
  • She is very analytical and not in-touch with her emotions (something I personally relate to)
  • She is a badass who can always tell someone off with affirmative wordplay
